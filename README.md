# DeepThought Target Company Research Assignment

## Objective

This project identifies and analyzes high-potential manufacturing companies in Hyderabad using DeepThought's target company research framework.

The goal is to create a research funnel from a broad company universe and identify the top Federer-fit companies based on manufacturing strength, leadership, growth potential, and operational maturity.

---

## Research Funnel

Initial Companies Researched: 100

Final Shortlisted Companies: 25

The companies include:
- Strong Fits
- Fit Companies
- Borderline Companies
- Weak / Failed Companies

---

## Eligibility Gates

### E1: Producer Gate
Checks whether the company builds/manufactures a real product.

### E2: Accessibility Gate
Checks whether the company is reachable and operationally accessible.

---

## Federer Scoring Framework

Companies were scored across:

| Criteria | Weight |
|---|---|
| C3 Differentiated Product | 20 |
| C4 Decision Maker Quality | 15 |
| C5 Market Tailwind | 15 |
| C6 Growth Signals | 15 |
| C7 Systems & Processes | 20 |
| C8 Succession / Leadership Depth | 15 |

Total Score: 100
